The Process

Made with
intention.

Every EasytoDecor product goes through a careful, hands-on process. No shortcuts, no mass production.

01
Design & Consultation
We start with your space in mind. Custom sizes, colour palettes, and finishes are discussed before a single drop of resin is poured.
02
Material Selection
Only food-safe, non-toxic epoxy resins and full-grain or vegetable-tanned leathers make the cut. Pigments are hand-blended for every pour.
03
Handcrafting
Resin pieces cure over 48–72 hours; leather is cut, edged, and saddle-stitched by hand. Every piece demands patience — and gets it.
04
Finishing & Quality Check
Surfaces are sanded through multiple grits and sealed to a glass finish. Every piece is inspected before it leaves the studio.
